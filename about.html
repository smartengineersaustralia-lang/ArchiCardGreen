Our story

ArchiGreenCard started in 2015 with a simple frustration: too many "modern" buildings in Nairobi ignored the climate they stood in — trapping heat, wasting water, and depending entirely on the grid.

We set out to build differently. Every plan starts with the sun path and the wind, not the other way around. A decade on, we've carried that same discipline from single family homes to twelve‑storey timber towers.

Today our studio works across Nairobi, Karen, and Westlands, with a growing list of clients who want buildings that perform as well as they look — and a team that answers on WhatsApp, not a call queue.
